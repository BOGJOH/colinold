For several years, the Rotary Club of Chichester and District had run a poetry competition but my school had never taken part. With my love of poetry, I decided to enter a number of pupils from my lunch-time poetry club. We were up against several other schools, including private schools, and pupils who were home-tutored. For five consecutive years, my pupils procured not only recommendations but top prizes and the resulting financial benefits.

Matt was a keen student but found English a difficult subject. Being a keen member of the Cadet Force in his Chichester school, he dreamt of joining the RAF. However, he needed a C-grade for English Language, but was only predicted a D. After 2 months of tuition, not only did he pass, but he exceeded all expectations and gained a B. He is currently training with the RAF.
