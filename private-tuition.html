Why Me

Over the past 30 years, I have privately tutored all manner of students, including both school pupils and adults. I had only ever anticipated tutoring children of school age for SATs exams (11 and 14-year-olds), GCSEs (16-year- olds) and entrance exams for private schools. However, after tutoring a man in the navy who needed an English qualification, and coaching a Spanish final year student for her re-takes, I decided to make myself available for adults as well.

I am happy to travel to local destinations and my hourly fee is very reasonable when compared to what other tutors or tuition centres are charging. Tutoring in a more relaxed environment, in familiar surroundings over a cup of coffee, is often more preferable to the usual more formal, impersonal setting of a school or college.
